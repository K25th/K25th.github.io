number 1

what is it about?

is about a 10 year old girl who gets trapped in a world of spirits after her parents are turned into pigs by a witch. To save them, she must find work in a bathhouse for gods and spirits, losing her name in the process, and learn to be independent and courageous to survive and return to her own world. number 2

what is it about?

One Piece Film: Red is about a world-famous singer named Uta who holds her first-ever live concert, which also serves as a front for a misguided plan to create a new world free of suffering. The Straw Hat Pirates, led by Luffy, attend the concert and discover that Uta is his former childhood friend and the daughter of "Red-Haired" Shanks. Uta plans to trap the world in an alternate reality using her "otherworldly" voice, and the pirates must stop her, while Shanks also plays a prominent role in the story.

GENRE:

animation,comedy,action and musical
